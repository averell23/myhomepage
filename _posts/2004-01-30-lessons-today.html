I'm still in the middle of writing my thesis. Some lessons I have learned over the last few days, in no particular order:

  • The main reason not to update your schedule is because you'd realize you are never going to make it in time.
  • This is the same reason why most people don't have a schedule at all.
  • Open doors are nice for people who want to come in. They are not so nice if you don't want to be disturbed.
  • Shared offices promote communication, not concetration.
